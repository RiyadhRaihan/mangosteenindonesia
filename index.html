PT Asasta Sentosa Abadi 

PT Asasta Sentosa Abadi is an exporter of the best quality mangosteen fruit in Indonesia, which has been operating since 2020 and domiciled in Denpasar, Bali.

Our company has a supply of mangosteen that spreads at many points in Indonesia with
the best quality for export.

We strongly believe in professionalism, commitment, openness, and hospitality, our company can provide the best service and cooperation that will be sustainable

Our Vision

We aim to become a trusted Indonesian mangosteen exporting company with the best service.

Our Mission

Focus on good service and maintain high quality control standards starting from processing at the farmer, sorting material quality, processing products, to delivery. We ensure our products are of the best quality. 

CORE VALUE

Integrity

Integrity means telling the truth, keeping our word, and treating others with fairness and respect. Integrity is one of our most cherished assets. It must not be compromised.

Quality

Quality is exhibited in many ways by selling and supporting products and services that delight customers, establishing a work with eco-friendly approach.

Commitment

Commitment is important where we will continue to provide the best to our customers all the time. Our opportunity to serve should be viewed as a privilege that is not to be taken for granted.

Innovation

Innovation means inventing, designing, and developing products and services that have high appeal in the marketplace. Eco-friendly innovation already applied.

Mangosteen Fruit

● Calories : 143
● Carbs : 35 grams
● Fiber : 3.5 grams

● Vitamin C : 9% of the Reference Daily Intake (RDI)
● Vitamin B9 (folate) : 15% of the RDI
● Vitamin B1 (thiamine) : 7% of the RDI
● Vitamin B2 (riboflavin) : 6% of the RDI
● Manganese : 10 % of the RDI
● Copper: 7% of the RDI
● Magnesium : 6% of the RDI
